niunai is an active cosplayer and dancer on bilibili
showing up at various only exhibitions with signings and fan interactions
i dont want to write about her history nor am i curious
after being exposed for having a boyfriend her fans exploded
burning merch doxxing harassment texts the full package
the announcements are all deleted now details cant be verified anymore
but the outline of what happened is clear enough
a creator who works independently treated as a traitor by her consumers for falling in love

i only started thinking backwards after this incident

why are these people so fucking mad
its just a content creator having a boyfriend
oh shes doing idol business idol business eating these peoples money
wait the money was given voluntarily
then why are fans so pressed
oh theres actual economic activity involved the signings at various small exhibitions


[well informed] (easy) success signing
at domestic acg offline events you can spend one or two hundred yuan for thirty seconds of physical close contact plus a polaroid with your name handwritten by her
two things make this act more than just consumption

first is market pricing
different people have different prices
not set by her arbitrarily but by the markets valuation of her popularity
this means theres a public quote for how much this person is worth
second is depth of contact
with the same person a photo cut is one price a special bonus is another and more private interactions go even higher
what you buy at higher prices isnt more time or better stuff but a stronger feeling of only i can get this close to you

these two things stacked together the structure itself implies a relationship not a transaction


dancer plus cosplayer plus only exhibition performer
these three identities stacked together create a very special kind of existence in chinas acg scene
shes not an idol but shes running her business using idol methods

this isnt a contradiction its the result of economic logic driving things
dancing and cosplay are hard to monetize on their own but the person herself can become a commodity
fans are willing to pay for physical contact with her
so the photo cut and only exhibition logic naturally grafts on

the problem is creators think theyre selling photos
fans think theyre raising a relationship
these two things were never the same thing from day one
the photo cut itself manufactures false intimacy
fans pay money gain physical closeness and the brain interprets this contact as a relationship rather than a transaction
this is a structural problem not niunais personal fault


the japanese idol industry is an interesting reference point
that system runs not because fans are high quality but because theres a pile of contracts backing it up
companies sign love ban clauses companies bear legal risks fans know the rules before they even enter

the domestic scene took the most valuable part pricing intimacy directly
then threw away everything else
no company no contracts no one backing anything up


the 2d idol subculture audience is fucking stupid

reading these peoples posts i only get one feeling my language module is broken
let me give a few examples

feel it
its weird
these people are serious when they say this stuff
not performing not memeing genuinely serious

they know how absurd this whole thing is but they need it

real life doesnt have these things
no camps no trials no feeling of standing on the right side
they consciously choose to consume a real person to give themselves a sense of existence

this isnt a stupidity unique to this circle
the photo cut system works not just because creators found a monetization method but also because theres genuine demand waiting on the fan side
relationships in real life are vague unstable unscripted
but here the rules are clear
you pay you get exclusivity you belong to a specific camp your emotional investment has a clear object

**this clarity is a scarce commodity**

so they werent deceived
they know what this is but they need the structure it provides
when the structure breaks like when the idol gets a boyfriend their anger isnt about being deceived but about that sense of structure suddenly vanishing
they want to regain control
the method is trials taking sides making things have right and wrong again

the creator side has no protection mechanisms
the fan side has unacknowledged emotional needs
these two things collided and a result has already come out

the conditions for the next incident are already in place
just hasnt reached the time yet
